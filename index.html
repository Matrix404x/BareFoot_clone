WHO IS THE BAREFOOT NOMAD?
We’re Micki and Charles

Two friendly Canadians who decided to quit their jobs back in 2003 and travel the world together. Since then we started a family, have visited dozens of countries and have helped thousands of people enjoy a life full of travel.

From visiting far off places to travel conferences to press trips to writing and photographing for numerous publications, we’ve had the opportunity to explore all aspects of travel.

We’ve also been fortunate to been featured on dozens of travel sites and continue to enjoy sharing our love of travel with the world.
